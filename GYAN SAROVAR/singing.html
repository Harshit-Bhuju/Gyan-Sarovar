Singing

At Gyan Sarovar, we are committed to providing a comprehensive arts education, and our singing class facility is a testament to this dedication. Our singing classes are held every Thursday, offering students a consistent and immersive experience to develop their vocal skills and musical understanding. Each session is led by accomplished and passionate instructors who are dedicated to nurturing the vocal talents of every student.

The Thursday singing classes at Gyan Sarovar are structured to be both engaging and educational. Our state-of-the-art music studio is equipped with advanced audio equipment, providing an ideal environment for vocal training and practice. Students are encouraged to explore various genres of music, helping them to find their unique voice and express themselves through song.
