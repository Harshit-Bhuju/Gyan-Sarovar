Dancing Classes

At Gyan Sarovar, we take pride in our exceptional dance program, which is a cornerstone of our holistic educational approach. Our dance classes are held every Thursday, providing students with a consistent and immersive experience that helps them develop their skills and passion for dance. Each session is led by experienced and enthusiastic instructors who are dedicated to nurturing the talent and creativity of every student. The curriculum covers a wide range of dance styles, from classical to contemporary, ensuring that students receive a well-rounded dance education. The Thursday dance classes at Gyan Sarovar are designed to be both engaging and educational. Our state-of-the-art dance studio is equipped with mirrored walls, high-quality sound systems, and ample space for movement, creating an ideal environment for learning and practice.
