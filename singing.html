Singing

At Gyan Sarovar, we are committed to providing a comprehensive arts education, and our singing class facility is a testament to this dedication. Our singing classes are held every Thursday, offering students a consistent and immersive experience to develop their vocal skills and musical understanding. Each session is led by accomplished and passionate instructors who are dedicated to nurturing the vocal talents of every student. The curriculum is designed to cover a wide range of musical styles, from classical to contemporary, ensuring a well-rounded vocal education.

The Thursday singing classes at Gyan Sarovar are structured to be both engaging and educational. Our state-of-the-art music studio is equipped with advanced audio equipment, providing an ideal environment for vocal training and practice. Students are encouraged to explore various genres of music, helping them to find their unique voice and express themselves through song. Our flexible yet structured approach ensures that students of all skill levels can participate and progress at their own pace, building confidence and honing their talents.

In addition to regular singing lessons, Gyan Sarovar's Thursday classes often feature special workshops and guest instructors, offering students the chance to learn from professionals in the music industry. These sessions provide valuable insights into advanced vocal techniques and current trends in music, inspiring students to push their boundaries and achieve new levels of excellence. Regular performances and showcases are also a key part of our program, allowing students to display their talents and gain performance experience. By making singing a weekly highlight, Gyan Sarovar ensures that students not only develop their vocal skills but also gain important life skills such as discipline, teamwork, and self-expression.
